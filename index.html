Welcome to Augmentix!

Augmentix is the premier club at the Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Department, dedicated to exploring and advancing the exciting fields of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science.

Join us on this journey of discovery, innovation, and collaboration. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a curious beginner, there's a place for you at Augmentix.

Vision

To develop a cooperative community of innovators at AUGMENTiX who are exploring and leading cutting-edge topics, inspiring research in technology and data-driven solutions

Mission

Our mission at AUGMENTiX is to give students who are enthusiastic in data science, artificial intelligence, and related subjects a vibrant platform. With hackathons, seminars, and group projects, we hope to provide a rich learning environment.
